About Me

Hello! My name is Pat.

I currently work for Patriot Fire Protection Inc. This company specializes in building fire sprinklers for residential and commercial buildings. My tasks consist of deciphering blueprints and listing materials required for construction. Once the material is listed and the pipe is ready for fabrication, I will then send the charge outs to accounting to be processed and billed to the job. Then the listing will be released to the annex for picking material and paperwork sent to the shop for further fabrication.
